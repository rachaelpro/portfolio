Hi! I'm Rachael.

I'm currently studying Comparative Literature and Business and Technical Writing at Rutgers University. In my free time, I enjoy hiking, reading, listening to music, and spending time with my cat and dog (pictured below). I have a wide range of academic interests, from IT to art history to chemistry, and writing is my favorite skill to practice.
I'm pursuing a career in which I can make the most of my skills and continue to do what I love. I’m interested in research because of the opportunity to put my strengths toward improving the world around me. You can read samples of my technical writing here. and my academic writing here.
Creative writing is important to me as a form of art and expression. I'm interested in capturing the experience of the world through someone else's eyes. Some topics I write about are family dynamics, home, and coming of age. Click here to read a few of my short stories. A dream of mine is to one day publish a novel. You can read the first five pages of a manuscript I wrote here.
I wanted my portfolio to be as personal as possible, so all the photos you see on this website are pictures I personally took. You can learn more about the stories behind some of the photos here.
I'm always looking for opportunities to learn more and put my skills to practice. Connect with me at rachaelprokap@gmail.com.
